# MOUNT FOG

is a multidisciplinary art project established in 2011  
by Nicola Domaneschi (b, 1987. Italy), Erich Grunewald (b, 1988. Sweden) and Marco Verdi (b, 1987. Italy).  
We are committed to photography and sound art.
